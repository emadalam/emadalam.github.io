atvjs Star

  • Blazing fast Apple TV application development using pure JavaScript
    The most popular Apple Tv application development framework using pure JavaScript. atvjs was the only one of its kind during the time of its inception and possibly still is. It utilizes the apple tv proprietary tvjs and tvml under the hood, while abstracting the intricacies to provide a clean and rapid app development framework.

persistme Star

  • localStorage on steroids :syringe:
    persistme lets you namespace your localStorage across multiple applications even when they are hosted on the same domain. It also has some built in goodies for common localStorage tasks and ~10x storage with built in compression.
